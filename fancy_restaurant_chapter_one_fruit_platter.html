Fancy Restaurant, Chapter One: #Fruit Platter
With the title ‘Fancy Restaurant, Chapter One: #Fruit Platter’, this project satirizes and questions the contradictions between people in the network and in reality through a combination of humorous and poetic words and various sculptural objects. By questioning a series of social phenomenons such as taking photos before meals, it reveals the fact that people relies on the “parallel self” in the digital world to “correct” the real world of life. Using hashtags from social platforms as the breakthrough element, it selects several top listed hashtags on instagram to involve in the narratives. The title ‘fruit platter’ metaphorizes a formalistic beauty that is deliberately created for visual effect.
